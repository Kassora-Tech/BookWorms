Why Secondhand?

New isn't always better. Sometimes it's just newer.

01

Kinder on the planet

Every book we resell is one less tree felled, one less truck run, one less story lost to a landfill. Shopping secondhand is the most radical act of love for the environment.

02

Kinder on your wallet

Read twice as much for the price of one. Great stories shouldn't cost the earth — literally or financially. Our prices start at R30.

03

Comes with character

A coffee ring. A pressed flower. A name in the front cover. A pencilled note in the margin. Every copy carries someone's fingerprints — and now it carries yours.
