Why Secondhand?

New isn't always better. Sometimes it's just newer.

Kinder on the planet

Every book we resell is one less tree felled, one less truck run, one less book in a landfill.

Kinder on your wallet

Read twice as much for the price of one. Great stories shouldn't cost what they used to.

Comes with character

A coffee ring. A pressed flower. A name in the front cover. Every copy carries someone's fingerprints.
